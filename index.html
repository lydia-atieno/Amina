Amina Abdi Rabar: Media Personality

Date Of Birth: 1stJanuary 1989

Gender: Female

Profession: Journalist

Amina Abdi Rabar is a vivacious soul who has established herself in Kenya's media industry. She is a TV and radio host, MC, musician and voice-over artist who has received numerous awards. Amina is among the top 100 most influential African women.
